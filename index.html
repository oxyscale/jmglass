What We Build
Your quoting system, end to end
A web-based application tailored to your product range, pricing structure, and quote format.
1
Job Type Selection
Pick from your 10 core job types: frameless showers, pool fences, balustrades, splashbacks, reglazes, mirrors, wardrobe doors, cabinet glass, and more. The form adapts to show only what's relevant.
2
Dynamic Configuration
Select glass type, thickness, and colour from filtered dropdowns. Enter dimensions. Pick hardware finishes. Toggle processing options (cutouts, polishing, beveling). Add coatings. Set labour hours.
3
Multi-Section Quotes
Complex jobs with multiple areas (e.g. outside balcony + inside void + staircase) are handled as separate sections within one quote. Each section configured independently, all rolling up into a single total.
4
Branded PDF Generation
One click generates a professional quote PDF matching your current format exactly. Company header, description of works (auto-written from your selections, fully editable), line items, GST calculation, and your standard conditions and exclusions.
5
Instant Email Delivery
Quote PDF emailed directly to the customer from the system. No downloading, no attaching, no switching between apps.
